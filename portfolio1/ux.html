NICU App

Many Neonatal Intensive Care Units(NICU) often have sound levels about APA standards(45 decibels). We identified one of the biggest noise sources as the alarms located next to the incubators. These alarms are vital because they tell nurses that something is wrong with the baby. However, the downside to these alarms is that they are very sensitive and they don't distinguish between false alarms vs true alarms. Our project solves this problem by relocating the alarms to a tablet assigned to the designated nurses.
