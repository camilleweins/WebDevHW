Background

AIChe is a chemical engineering competition in which university teams build a small car that runs off of chemical reactions. The first part of the competition is a poster design qualifier. My job was to design an eye-catching poster that met the AIChe guidelines. In addition, I made the schematic diagrams for the poster.
