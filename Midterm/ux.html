The Problem

The NICU incubators do not provide optimal noise canceling features to reduce sound to 45dB or lower for the premature babies. The sound levels in the NICU are above APA standards (40 dB maximum). We identified the primary users as the nurses.

  • Nurses
  • Doctors

    • User Research

    Because these nurses are busy, simplifying the interface was our big concern. Our app needed to “feel” like the monitor they use in the UCLA NICU so the nurses wouldn’t have to relearn how to read the information on the screen. Another key point was that we didn’t want to replace the actual monitor, just make it so alarms were closer to the user. Originally, we wanted to make this a wearable, however, we realized that the screen size is too small. Through a few more interviews, we noticed that “Rachel” kept mentioning “a tablet.”
